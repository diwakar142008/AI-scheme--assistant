# AI-Powered Government Scheme Awareness & Eligibility Assistant

## üìå Problem Statement
India has numerous government welfare schemes for students, farmers, women,
senior citizens, MSMEs, and low-income citizens. However, many eligible people
do not benefit from these schemes due to lack of awareness, complex eligibility
rules, scattered information, and low digital literacy. The problem is not the
absence of schemes, but the difficulty in accessing and understanding them.

---

## üí° Proposed Solution
This project proposes an AI-powered assistant that helps citizens discover
relevant government schemes, check eligibility, and understand application
procedures in simple language. Users provide minimal inputs such as age group,
occupation, income range, and location. The system matches this information
with scheme eligibility rules and provides personalized recommendations.

---
## How the AI System Works

The AI-powered Government Scheme Assistant uses a rule-based and NLP-assisted approach to help citizens identify relevant government welfare schemes based on their personal and socio-economic details.

1. *User Input Collection*  
   Users provide basic information such as age, gender, education level, income range, occupation, location (state), and social category through a simple interface or chatbot.

2. *Government Scheme Dataset*  
   The system uses structured datasets containing details of central and state government schemes, including eligibility criteria, benefits, and target beneficiaries. Initial data is stored in JSON format and can be expanded using open government portals.

3. *Eligibility Matching Logic*  
   The AI engine compares user inputs with scheme eligibility conditions using rule-based filters and keyword matching. Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques help interpret both user queries and scheme descriptions.

4. *Recommendation Engine*  
   Based on matching scores, the system ranks and recommends the most relevant schemes to the user along with clear explanations of why they are eligible.

5. *Scalability and Future AI Enhancements*  
   In future versions, machine learning models can be integrated to improve accuracy, handle incomplete data, support multiple Indian languages, and provide personalized recommendations.

The solution relies on publicly available and ethically sourced data to ensure transparency, accuracy, and reusability.

  - Data Type: Scholarship schemes, eligibility criteria, benefits
